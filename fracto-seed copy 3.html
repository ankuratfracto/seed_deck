    Solution Slide 03

    Closed-loop, agentic DocumentOps

    One queue, four stages, full telemetry. Docs land, validators run, recon bots clear, and systems update inside 30 seconds.

    Product pillars

    • Residency-ready VPC ingestion (email/API/WhatsApp) with queueing + policy routing.
    • Validator studio with BFSI/logistics guardrails → ≥90% straight-through processing.
    • Write-back automations for LOS/ERP/TMS with immutable audit trails.

    Agentic runbook

    1. Read & classify — single queue across channels.
    2. Validate — policy/compliance/LLM validators enforce guardrails.
    3. Reconcile — recon bots resolve 90%+ exceptions.
    4. Write-back — systems update with replayable telemetry.
