## Voiceover Script

Driver and vehicle onboarding should not slow down a mobility business.

But in high-volume operations, Aadhaar, PAN, driving license, and RC uploads often arrive blurred, incomplete, or inconsistent.

Teams spend hours retyping fields, checking records across tabs, masking identity data manually, and pushing low-quality documents into review queues.

Fracto changes that.

Fracto is an AI infrastructure layer for mobility onboarding and fleet operations.

Drivers or ops teams upload Aadhaar, PAN, DL, and RC documents through your app, admin tool, or batch workflow.

Fracto classifies the document, extracts the right fields, masks sensitive Aadhaar data, validates records through Parivahan checks, and returns structured JSON with confidence flags and audit-ready status.

Clean records can move straight to approval.

Low-confidence cases are routed to reviewers, so your team spends time only where human judgment is needed.

Fracto fits into your existing stack through REST APIs, webhook callbacks, dashboards, and reviewer queues.

The result: less manual data entry, faster driver activation, stronger compliance, and one unified layer for parsing and validation.

Start with a focused two-week pilot across Aadhaar, PAN, DL, and RC workflows.

Fracto. Automate mobility onboarding from document upload to verified decision.
