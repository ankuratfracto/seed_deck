    What “hidden tax” covers

    667 analyst hours/month just to read & key 10k docs/month plus 4% QA loops that spawn SLA penalties and re-audits. Double the volume and the rupee burn doubles because the system is almost fully linear.

    • 10,000 documents/month × 4 min manual handling = 40,000 min ≈ 667 work‑hours/month

    • ~4% error‑rate on critical data → ripple cost to audits/regulators

    Footnote: Assumes 4 minutes per document and ₹300/hour; scales linearly by volume. Example: 10k/month ≈ 667 hours/month → ₹2 L/month (~₹24 L/year).
