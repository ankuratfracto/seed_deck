The operations gap
Why this matters

Freight forwarders do not lose margin on one task. They lose it across every handoff.

After confirmation, work splits across emails, portals, PDFs, TMS screens, and manual follow-ups.

Cutoffs move faster than inboxes

SI, VGM, gate-in, docs, and customs cutoffs sit in scattered messages.

Operators re-key the same facts

Ports, parties, containers, weights, references, and contacts get copied again and again.

Exceptions are invisible until late

Missing invoices, BL mismatches, and portal failures show up too late.
