Why E-commerce Ops Break Down
The Operational Problem

Parcel operations still depend on manual visual checks and document-heavy exception handling.

Quick-commerce and e-commerce teams move fast, but proof verification, billing, and export compliance still get stuck in queues.

Delivery Proof

Rider image uploads are inconsistent

Teams need to know if the photo is a real package proof, not a random or unusable image.

Returns

Damaged and wrong-item claims are hard to verify

Photos, labels, package state, and remarks need consistent checks before refund or escalation.

Billing

Invoices and shipment documents are re-keyed

Manual extraction slows reconciliation, seller payouts, and customer billing.

Compliance

Export files need repeated checks

LUT, AD Code, IEC, invoices, and supporting documents need validation before dispatch or clearance.

Typical failure pattern
  • Riders upload blurry, cropped, duplicate, or irrelevant package photos
  • Ops teams manually open images to confirm package validity
  • Invoice, AWB, POD, and manifest data is copied across tools
  • Export-document gaps are found late in the shipment workflow
What e-commerce teams need instead
  • Automated valid/invalid image decisions in the delivery flow
  • Document extraction for invoices, PODs, manifests, and AWBs
  • Rules and validation for LUT, AD Code, IEC, and invoice bundles
  • Manual review only for low-confidence or policy-sensitive cases
