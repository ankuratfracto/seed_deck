    Solution 04 · Solution

    Solution — Closed-Loop, Agentic DocumentOps

    One queue, four stages, full telemetry — docs go from inbox to system update in ≤30 seconds.

    Product

    Agentic DocumentOps OS

    • One inbox for all documents (email, API, WhatsApp).
    • AI reads documents and extracts clean, structured data.
    • Policy rules check limits, compliance, and basic fraud flags.
    • Bots update LOS/ERP/TMS and keep a full audit trail.
    How it works

    4-step closed loop

    1. 1
      Capture

      All documents land in a single, prioritised queue.

    2. 2
      Validate

      AI validates data and business rules.

    3. 3
      Reconcile

      Bots resolve issues, route edge cases to humans.

    4. 4
      Write-back

      Core systems write back and every step is logged.
