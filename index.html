    Problem 02 · Problem

    Problem — Manual DocumentOps Tax Your Margins

    Every 10k docs costs ~₹2.55L in hidden manual work.

    Lenders and logistics teams still hand-key critical docs across inboxes, portals, and spreadsheets.

    Why teams hurt

    • Loan underwriting: 7–12 documents per case (bank statements, GST, ITRs, PoDs) keyed by hand before LOS sees anything.
    • Logistics PoDs and LR notes: 10–15 min / document across scanning, lookup, update in TMS/ERP.
    • 4–8% leakage triggers SLA fines and re-audits.

    Hidden tax per 10k docs

    StepCost
    Reading + keying (4 min)₹2L / month
    Error rework (4%)₹40k / month
    SLA hits & audits₹15k / month
    Total drag₹2.55L / month

    At 100k docs/month → ₹25L burn and SLA risk — per customer.

    What We Do — Agentic DocumentOps OS

    Residency-first, closed-loop IDP for BFSI/logistics with validators and write-back in the box.

    One queue, four stages, full telemetry — inbox to system update in ≤30 seconds.

    Product

    Agentic DocumentOps OS

    • Unified inbox for email, APIs, WhatsApp, uploads.
    • Vision-language extraction tuned for BFSI/logistics docs.
    • Policy + recon packs targeting >95% STP.
    • Native LOS/ERP/TMS write-back with full audit trails.
    How it works

    4-step closed loop

    1. 1
      Capture

      All channels into one queue; auto-classify and de-dupe.

    2. 2
      Understand & extract

      Vision–language models read docs with per-field confidence.

    3. 3
      Validate & reconcile

      Policy packs, checks vs LOS/ERP/TMS; only true exceptions surface.

    4. 4
      Write-back

      Auto-update LOS/ERP/TMS with full audit log.
