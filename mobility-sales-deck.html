Why Mobility Ops Break Down
The Operational Problem

Driver and vehicle onboarding is still document-heavy, slow, and error-prone.

Mobility operators move fast, but onboarding still gets stuck in manual review and poor-quality uploads.

Driver KYC

Identity capture fails in the field

Blurred Aadhaar and PAN uploads slow approvals.

Vehicle Ops

DL and RC data is retyped manually

Retyping creates errors and delays.

Risk

Fraud checks arrive too late

Weak validation pushes bad records downstream.

Scale

Volume spikes crush reviewer bandwidth

Launches and hiring pushes create instant backlogs.

Typical failure pattern
  • Mixed-quality Aadhaar, PAN, DL, and RC uploads
  • Raw OCR without normalization or validation
  • Manual reviewer cleanup across multiple tabs
  • Slow approvals and lower conversion
What mobility teams need instead
  • One layer for extraction, masking, and validation
  • Manual review only on low-confidence cases
  • Fast integration into apps and admin tools
  • Clear auditability for compliance teams
