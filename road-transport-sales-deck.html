Why Transport Ops Break Down
The Operational Problem

Transport document review is still manual, repetitive, and easy to miss.

PODs, hire letters, and trip sheets often need more than OCR. Teams also need proof that the right signoff is actually there.

POD

Signoff checks are manual

Teams still inspect whether a POD is signed, stamped, or annotated.

Exceptions

Handwritten notes get missed

Delivery remarks and shortages often sit in handwritten sections.

Finance

Trip sheets and hire docs need structure

Core fields still get keyed manually for matching and review.

Scale

Proof queues pile up fast

Backlogs build when every document needs a human visual check.

Typical failure pattern
  • PODs and trip sheets arrive in mixed quality
  • Teams check stamps and signatures by eye
  • Handwritten remarks are missed or delayed
  • Proof completion slows billing and follow-up
What operators need instead
  • Fast parsing plus visual presence checks
  • Flags for missing signoff or missing stamp
  • Handwritten remark detection for exceptions
  • Review only the uncertain cases
