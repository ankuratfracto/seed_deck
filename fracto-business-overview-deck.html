Fracto Business Overview 01
Fracto
Why Teams Need Fracto
The Operational Problem

Critical workflows still move through inboxes, spreadsheets, and approval threads.

OCR alone creates extracted text. Operations teams need validated data, exception handling, approvals, and controlled updates into their systems.

Intake

Documents arrive from everywhere

Email, portals, WhatsApp, APIs, and batch uploads create scattered intake.

Review

Teams re-key and chase approvals

Manual checks slow SLAs and create avoidable rework.

Risk

Audit evidence is fragmented

Approvals and exceptions live in folders, chats, and email trails.

Scale

Cost grows with every new file

Volume spikes require more reviewers instead of better automation.

Typical failure pattern
  • Documents land in many channels
  • Fields are typed into spreadsheets or core systems
  • Exceptions are handled in email or chat
  • Audit trail is rebuilt later during reviews
What buyers need instead
  • One accountable workflow run per case
  • Structured extraction with confidence and validations
  • Reviewer queues only for low-confidence exceptions
  • APIs and webhooks into existing business systems

Operating Model

Fracto sits inside existing business workflows without forcing a system rip-and-replace.

01
Receive
Documents enter through a product app, customer portal, mailbox, API, or batch source.
02
Process
Fracto extracts, validates, and flags exceptions with structured outputs.
03
Review
Only unresolved cases move to reviewers with evidence and comments.
04
Return
Decisions, fields, and audit status flow back to the system of record.
Integration options
  • REST APIs for synchronous workflows
  • Webhook callbacks for async document jobs
  • Dashboard and reviewer queues for operations teams
  • Custom field mapping into customer or internal systems
Deployment expectations
  • Workflow discovery and sample document review
  • Parser and rule configuration for the first use case
  • Security, access, and audit requirements aligned upfront
  • Production rollout after measurable workflow proof
